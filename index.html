Why choose us?

Now more than ever, detailed disinfecting methods should be in place to protect the guests and employees of your facility. A clean and healthy environment is essential for all businesses and facilities.

Simple and Flexible booking system

While other cleaning services charge per area or item, we do prefer the ”per hour & per cleaner” fee. This allows our clients to maximize the cleaning hours efficiently and lets them have their whole space deeply cleaned.

Powerful and hotel-like cleaning

Our team only uses modern cleaning vacuum & steaming machines all straight from Germany. With this cleaning equipment, we get rid of allergens, dust mites, bacteria, and other unreachable dirt which delivers not just dirt free but a healthy living environment.

Professional and efficient cleaners

Our cleaners undergo a series of training to assure the quality of cleaning service. From traditional techniques to modern ways of cleaning, we give situational training which makes them well equipped and knowledgeable. We also do background checks to guarantee clients’ safety

Environment and pet friendly

We don’t use any chemicals in cleaning! No muriatic acid or any harmful cleaning agent. Our secret? Just purely water! And that’s thanks to our modern cleaning machines!
